About Me

Data tells a story. I make sure it's heard.

I'm a self-motivated Data Scientist currently pursuing my Master's in Data Science at the Università di Napoli Federico II, Italy. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, statistical analysis, and product thinking.

I don't just build models — I build things people use. During the Apple Developer Academy at Federico II, I shipped iOS apps through the full lifecycle: ideation, design, development, and TestFlight beta testing with real users.

Originally from Jaipur, Rajasthan, I've built a career on curiosity — whether that's exploring a new dataset or a new city.
